About Me

Hello! My name is Jason Blake. I have nearly 20 years experience in the food & beverage/hospitality industry. I'm currently a driver with Uber while attending the coding bootcamp at UCI.

In my free time I enjoy art, music and the outdoors. A few of my favorite styles of music are techno, house and breakbeat. Psychedelic and Graffiti art are very interesting to me because of their vibrancy and multitude of styles. Hiking in the Angeles National Forrest is my prefered way to decompress from the hustle and bustle of life in a big city.

I hope this gives some insight about me. Feel free to ask me any questions or to just chat it up.
